How to Register

Follow the steps below to join the Spartan Track Club!

USATF Membership is required to officially be a part of Spartan Track Club Hawaii and to run in USATF sanctioned track meets. Membership is good for one year and prices are as follows:
  • 18 Years and Younger: $25 per Calendar Year
  • Adults: $40 per Calendar Year
  • VIP Membership for Any Age: $150 per Calendar Year
Step One: Register for USATF

  • Click the button to go to the USATF Website (open in new tab)
    • Go to USATF
  • Choose your desired plan, then click the JOIN button
  • You will be brought to a form, so fill out your personal information. When you reach the Club Info page, input your zip code and select “Spartan Track Club Hawaii” or “39-0523” in the Club Affiliation section. Finish the remainder of the form, then click to Check Out.
  • You will be prompted to agree to the USATF rules, policies, and agreements. Then you are able to pay for the membership. That’s it, you are registered for Spartan Track Club Hawaii!
