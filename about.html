About Us

Our Story

The Spartan Track Club was established in 2019 to promote and grow the sport of track and field in Hawaii. We sought to provide runners of all levels with opportunities to improve their times, form, and athletic capability. Initially, our team primarily consisted of sprinters along with a few distance runners. Since then, our team has expanded to include athletes in almost every event. Our Level I and II USATF certified coaches work to provide optimal training for our athletes to ensure they succeed in any event. They have extensive experience guiding and preparing runners, throwers, and jumpers of all skill levels. Read more about our team below.

Michael Harvey

Assistant Coach

Coach Harvey, grew up in Waco, TX playing football and running track. He earned a football scholarship to New Mexico State University, while also competing in track, and even rugby while at NMSU. Coach Harvey was the 1994 Big West Conference champion in the 100m and has played in the CFL as well as Team USA Rugby 7s. He moved from Texas to Hawaii in 2014 with his wife and daughter Malia Rose (Maryknoll c/2025). He began volunteering with the Maryknoll Track program in 2018. Coach Harvey is a USATF Level II Sprints coach and works with several top sprinters across Oahu. Coach Harvey still competes, in fact representing Team USA Masters team at the World Championships - Malaga Spain 2019 and was the 2019 USATF Indoor 60m National Champ age 40-44. He works in IT at a local airline company on Oahu.
